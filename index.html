Welcome to Siddartha Institute of Science and Technology

An emerging center of excellence is promoted by Jaya Educational Society. Founded and professionally run by distinguished academicians, medical professionals and technocrats belonging to Chittoor District of Andhra Pradesh. It is located at puttur town a place 35 KM away from Tirupati. This place is very near to the famous Narayanavanam, a holy place where lord Venkateswara married goddess Padmavathi and is well connected by both railway and road ways (Chennai - Tirupathi highway).
With strong ambition, it hait has over the years produced many brilliant engineering minds who have gone ahead and made a mark for themselves and the country. The college has the distinction of being affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Anantapur , A.P and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi .
Our objective for every student is to graduate with a degree, an impressive resume and be fully equipped with knowledge and skills needed to succeed in national and international workplace. .png We invite you to explore our campus, our teaching process, laboratories and facilities, and simply discover why SISTK stands as the most preferred destination for students who aspire to grow, learn and reach beyond the country.
